1. Business Goals

Business Goal Why It Matters North-Star Metric
1. Improve Customer Retention Renewal risk is currently caught too late to act on. Detecting it earlier protects ARR from churning. NRR: 105% → 115%
2. Improve Operating Margin CSMs spend ~5 min/account on manual cross-tool triage. Automating that triage reclaims capacity without adding headcount. Cost-to-Serve per Account

Product Roadmap KPIs Roll Up to Business Goals

Business Goal 1: Improve Customer Retention (North-Star: NRR 105% → 115%)

  • Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV)
  • ARR at Risk / ARR Protected

Business Goal 2: Improve Operating Margin (North-Star: Cost-to-Serve per Account)

  • CSM: Account Ratio (capacity/efficiency)
  • CSAT / Time-to-Resolution (quality proxy)
  • Engineering ROI

2. Product Objectives

Product Objective Supports Goal Target
Detect churn risk earlier Retention Surface newly at-risk accounts within each weekly digest cycle instead of only at renewal, RAG-grounded and source-cited, not a predictive forecast
Cut manual CSM triage time Margin Reduce triage to <2 min per account (from ~20 min)
Tie the engineering effort to the revenue impact Retention + Margin Ground roadmap items in a structured, cited, portfolio-wide demand signal instead of ad hoc/anecdotal requests
