1. Problem Statement

Who Experiences It

Dedicated Customer Success (CS) teams, CS leaders, Product Managers, and Engineering leads within Enterprise B2B SaaS organizations managing high-value, recurring revenue accounts.

Current Pains & Consequences

•

Revenue Churn:

High-value accounts slip away unexpectedly, wiping out quarters of sales pipeline and damaging Net Revenue Retention (NRR).

•

Information Fragmentation:

Vital health signals are siloed across disconnected systems (Salesforce, Zendesk, Stripe, Mixpanel, and error logs).

•

Extreme Manual Overhead:

CSMs lose 6–8 hours weekly compiling reports manually instead of proactively managing relationships.

•

Lack of Explanations:

Traditional "red/yellow/green" indicators are opaque, offering no root-cause analysis or guidance.

•

Misaligned Prioritization:

Product and Engineering fix bugs and construct roadmaps in isolation from the actual ARR at risk.

Existing Alternatives & Inadequacies

Legacy Customer Success platforms rely on rigid, rule-based scoring models. These alternatives are inadequate because they are backward-looking and offer no plain-English context or prescriptive advice. By the time a CSM notices a declining health score, the customer is already evaluating competitors.

Triggers & Context

The problem spikes during critical milestones—such as 90 days before renewal—or immediately following silent service disruptions (e.g., localized API latency spikes or silent feature abandonment).

4. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Enterprise Customer Success (CS) teams have long struggled with fragmented customer data scattered across CRMs, product analytics, support tickets, and billing portals. Pulse AI solves this challenge by acting as an intelligent decision layer that automatically ingests and correlates these multi-system signals. Utilizing an advanced, explainable Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture, the platform replaces vague health scores with clear, root-cause analyses and stakeholder-specific "next-best-action" recommendations.

5. THE PROBLEM
Siloed and Fragmented Customer Data

Customer signals are scattered across disconnected systems (CRM, product analytics, Zendesk, billing, and error logs). Because there is no single source of truth, organizations miss critical early warning signs of account distress, leading to unexpected, high-value churn that directly damages quarterly bookings.

Vague, Opaque Health Scores

Traditional CS platforms generate generic "red/yellow/green" health indicators without context. Without knowing why an account is flagged, Customer Success Managers (CSMs) must spend hours manually digging through support tickets and usage metrics, delaying critical outreach until the customer has already decided to leave.

Misaligned Cross-Functional Prioritization

Product and Engineering teams build roadmaps and fix bugs based on technical severity or gut feel rather than business impact. Because they lack visibility into how specific technical glitches affect high-ARR accounts, critical revenue-impacting issues remain unresolved while low-impact tasks are prioritized.

6. THE SOLUTION

Pulse AI acts as a unified decision platform that addresses these pain points by consolidating scattered data into real-time, explainable intelligence.

For CS Managers

Replaces 6 to 8 hours of manual weekly report generation with instant, plain-English account summaries and ready-to-send outreach plans, increasing team productivity.

For Product & Eng

Ranks open bugs, feature requests, and system errors by the total ARR of the affected accounts, allowing teams to align development priorities directly with revenue protection.

For Leadership

Automatically surfaces expansion opportunities and provides executives with accurate, data-backed renewal forecasts and revenue-at-risk alerts.

7. HOW IT WORKS

Pulse AI is built on a modern low-code/no-code UI and a secure Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) workflow powered by advanced Large Language Models.

1 Onboarding & Integrations

Non-technical administrators connect Pulse AI to existing enterprise tools (Salesforce, Zendesk, Stripe, and Mixpanel) in minutes using pre-built API connectors—requiring no complex data pipeline engineering.

2 Workflow & AI Reasoning

Once connected, the platform continuously ingests technical, financial, and engagement signals. When an anomaly is detected (such as a sudden drop in core feature adoption paired with an open billing dispute), the RAG workflow retrieves relevant historical records to analyze the threat.

3 Explainability & Outputs

Instead of presenting a black-box percentage score, Pulse AI generates an interactive dashboard. Users receive a cited, natural-language account summary explaining why the account is at risk (e.g., "API response times increased by 40% for the customer's primary workflow, causing a 15% drop in daily active users").

4 Recommendations

The system generates role-specific action cards. A CSM receives a drafted "save-play" email template addressing the technical issue, while the Engineering Lead receives an automated Jira ticket prioritized by the account's ARR value.
