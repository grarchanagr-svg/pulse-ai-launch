1. Detailed Primary Persona

Sarah Jenkins is the primary persona for PulseAI. She represents the Customer Success leader who needs earlier churn visibility, better operational leverage, and explainable AI insights.

Sarah Jenkins

VP of Customer Success

Age: 42
Company Size: 250 Employees
Industry: Enterprise B2B SaaS
Experience: 15+ Years in CS

“We are constantly fighting fires instead of building relationships because our health scores lag behind actual customer sentiment.”

Psychographics & Mindset

Highly protective of ARR metrics. Stressed by quarterly executive reviews where unexpected churn pops up. Deeply values proactive strategy but feels weighed down by reactive day-to-day crises.

Behaviors

Spends the first 2 hours of every morning reviewing high-value accounts. Constantly requests custom usage data pulls from data science teams to double-check structural health indicators.

Motivations

Achieve net revenue retention (NRR) targets exceeding 115%. Build a highly efficient CS operational setup capable of handling a larger volume of accounts without doubling headcount.

Unmet Needs

An early-warning indicator system that picks up on semantic anxiety and support ticket fatigue signals before traditional software usage drop-offs manifest.

Tech Comfort

Comfortable with SaaS tools, dashboards, and workflow automation.

AI Sentiment

Positive toward AI if outputs are explainable and tied to source data.

1. Business Goals

Business Goal Why It Matters North-Star Metric
1. Improve Customer Retention Renewal risk is currently caught too late to act on. Detecting it earlier protects ARR from churning. NRR: 105% → 115%
