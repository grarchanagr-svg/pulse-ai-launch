3. Addressing Stakeholder Concerns

Pushback 1: "AI is too expensive and token costs are unpredictable."
Rebuttal: We aren't training foundational models. By utilizing structured API architectures, setting hard budget caps, and only parsing essential metadata, our scaling runtime costs stay manageable—accounting for less than 18% of the total budget.
Pushback 2: "What is the concrete ROI? AI projects often stall in R&D."
Rebuttal: This initiative maps directly to customer retention metrics. Based on our dynamic framework adjustments, saving just a handful of mid-market accounts from churning completely covers the operational baseline costs.
Pushback 3: "Why build this now? Can't our team just look at standard dashboards?"
Rebuttal: Legacy metrics show historical trends after a user has emotionally checked out. PulseAI works ahead of the curve, detecting semantic frustration signals in support chains proactively before standard churn timelines lock in.
